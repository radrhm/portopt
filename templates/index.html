Historical Cumulative Returns
Tracks how $1 invested at the start would have grown for each asset and the blended portfolio (bold white line). The dotted grey line is the SPY benchmark. Hover to compare returns on any date.
Portfolio Drawdown
Percentage decline from the most recent portfolio peak at each point in time. A drawdown of −20% means the portfolio was 20% below its prior high. Shallower and shorter drawdowns indicate better downside protection.
Rolling Metrics
Shows how volatility (blue, left axis) and the Sharpe ratio (gold, right axis) evolved through the selected period using a sliding window. A rising Sharpe with falling volatility signals consistently improving risk-adjusted performance.
Correlation Matrix
Pairwise correlation of daily returns between each asset (−1 to +1). Dark red = move in opposite directions (great diversification), dark green = move together (concentration risk). Aim for a mix of low or negative correlations to reduce portfolio volatility.
Efficient Frontier
Each point on the blue curve is a possible portfolio — the curve shows the maximum return achievable for each level of risk. The gold star is your optimal portfolio. The green dashed line (Capital Allocation Line) shows the best risk/reward trade-off including a risk-free asset.
Risk & Performance Metrics
Comprehensive breakdown across four groups: Performance (return, Sharpe, Sortino), Drawdown & Tail (worst losses, duration), Value at Risk (probability-based daily loss estimates), and Benchmark (how the portfolio compares to SPY on beta, alpha, and tracking).

Monte Carlo Simulation
Simulates thousands of possible future portfolio paths by sampling from historical return distributions. The shaded band covers the 5th–95th percentile range of outcomes. This shows the spread of plausible scenarios — not a forecast.
Configure horizon & runs above, then click Run Simulation.
Stress Test — Historical Scenarios
Replays your current portfolio weights through six major historical market crises to estimate how it would have performed. Compares against SPY to show whether your diversification would have helped during real drawdowns.
Click Run Stress Test to simulate how this portfolio would have performed in past crises.
